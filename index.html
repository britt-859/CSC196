What is your birth flower?

The other day I learned from a friend that each month has a birth flower assigned to it. This webpage shows the birth flower for each month below.

January

Carnations and Snowdrop

The birth flower for January is the carnation and snowdrop. Carnations represent admiration, hope, love and rebirth.

February

Violet and Primrose

The birth flower for February is violet and primrose. Violets represent modesty, faithfullness, and love.

March

Daffodil

The birth flower for March is the daffodil. Daffodils represent new beginnings and prosperity.

April

Daisy and Sweet Pea

The birth flower for April is daisies and sweet peas. Daisies represent purity, innocence and pleasure.

May

Lily of the Valley and Hawthorn

The birth flower for May is lily of the valley and hawthorn. Lily of the valley represents sweetness, motherhood and hope.

June

Rose and Honeysuckle

The birth flower for June is rose and honeysuckle. Roses represent romance and happiness.

July

Larkspur and Water Lily

The birth flower for July is larkspur and water lilies. Larkspurs represent positivty, dignity and purity.

August

Gladiolus and Poppy

The birth flower for August is gladiolus and poppy. Gladiolus represents strength and imagination.

September

Aster and Morning Glory

The birth flower for Septermber is aster and morning glories. Aster represents love, affection and mortality.

October

Marigold and Cosmos

The birth flower for October is marigold and cosmos. Marigolds represent creativty, passion, peace and tranquility.

November

Chrysanthemum

The birth flower for November is chrysanthemum. Chrysanthemums represent loyalty and honesty.

December

Narcissus and Holly

The birth flower for December is narcissus and holly. Narcissus represent hope, wealth, protection and defense.
